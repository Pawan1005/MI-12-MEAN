Earth day cycle due to the effect its rotation and sun's position.

Sunrise

Sunrise (or sunup) is the moment when the upper rim of the Sun appears on the horizon in the morning. The term can also refer to the entire process of the solar disk crossing the horizon and its accompanying atmospheric effects.

Noon

Afternoon is the time of the day between noon and evening. ... It is the time when the sun is descending from its peak in the sky to somewhat before its terminus .

Sunset

Sunset, also known as sundown, is the daily disappearance of the Sun below the horizon due to Earth's rotation.

Night

the time from dusk to dawn when no sunlight is visible.
